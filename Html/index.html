About-us

Sharing Cohorts is a non-profit organization that is full of inspirational and Empathetic people,
The organization is created by a group of inspired youths who want the well-being of others.
We are dedicated to serving the needy and making a positive impact in our communities.
Our mission is to provide support, resources, and assistance to those in need,
and to empower individuals to improve their lives and the lives of those around them.
We believe that everyone deserves a helping hand, and we are committed to making a difference in the world.
Our team is made up of passionate volunteers who are dedicated to our cause.
